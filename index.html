What's inside

Built for
serious
aspirants

Every feature exists to solve one problem: the gap between knowing what to study and actually executing it, day after day, until the exam.
🧠
Adaptive AI Planner

Generates topic-specific tasks with reasons — not "Study Physics 45 min." Knows your coaching test dates, PYQ weightage, weak topics, and available hours.

👤
Student Profile

One-time setup: exam, date, coaching, target score, mock scores, weak subjects, sleep, stress, blocked slots. Every plan adapts to you.

📅
Daily Check-In

Every morning: select today's topics from the official NTA syllabus. Rate yesterday. Log wellbeing. Plan generates with full context.

📋
Coaching Schedule Upload

Paste your coaching schedule — Groq extracts test dates, subjects, chapters. Planner always knows what's coming in the next 7 days.

⏱️
Attention Cycle + Pause

30-min focus → 5-min break → 10-min long break. Floating overlay bar. Real pause/resume — stats track actual focus time, not wall clock time.

🚫
WorkMode Blocker

Blocks distracting apps during sessions using the Accessibility API. No root needed. You choose which apps get blocked.

💬
WhatsApp Guardian Reports

Automatically sends session summaries to a parent/guardian. Runs in an isolated process — survives even if the main app is killed.

🤖
Mentor AI

Chat with an AI that knows your full situation. Real guidance — chapter difficulty, misconceptions, spaced repetition, burnout detection. Not motivation quotes.

📊
Behavior Ledger + Stats

Skip rate, streaks, pause patterns, attention quality. Behavior data feeds back into the planner and mentor for adaptive adjustments over time.

🔑
BYOK — Your Keys

Supports Groq, OpenRouter, Gemini, Claude. Bring your own API key — no subscription, no data sent to our servers. Full control.

🔊
Voice Feedback (TTS)

Android TTS built-in. ElevenLabs as optional upgrade. Planner speaks your plan summary after generation. Toggle in settings.

🌙
Offline Fallback

LLM call fails? The rule-based planner kicks in automatically so you always get a plan, even without internet.
